Dr. Ruli Gao comes from The University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center, joining the Center for Computational Biology and Bioinformatics. Dr. Gao has pioneered single cell cancer genomics, where she applied both single cell DNA and RNA sequencing technologies and computational methods to track tumor evolution, delineate therapeutic resistance, dissect tumor microenvironment and develop novel single cell sequencing data analysis tools. The Gao lab is dedicated to translate complex human genome into data into new insights in cancer and cardiovascular disease.
